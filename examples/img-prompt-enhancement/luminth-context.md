# Luminth.ai Landing Page — Full Brand Context

## Tagline
"Most tools help you write a world. Luminth helps you run one."

## What Luminth Is
A modular, AI-assisted world-building and interactive storytelling platform for Game Masters, DMs, writers, world-builders, and indie game developers.

## Three Modes
- **Build** — Author worlds with AI-assisted generation. Entity editors, canon tools, timeline management.
- **Test** — Validate coherence before players find the gaps. Consistency checks, scenario simulations, NPC interaction tests.
- **Play** — Step into your world. Run multi-NPC scenes with characters that remember. Session tools, story state tracking, consequences that stick.

## Key Features
- **Modular Engines** — NPCs, settings, worlds, factions — each domain is its own engine with specialized tools.
- **AI as Collaborator** — AI proposes, you decide. All AI output is staged as draft, never auto-published.
- **Canonical Memory** — Explicit, queryable truth layer. Distinguish canon from belief from rumor. Append-only — worlds evolve, never rewrite.
- **NPCs That Remember** — Persistent memory, personality, relationships. They recall past encounters.
- **Local-First Ownership** — Worlds live on your infrastructure. Cloud optional, never required.
- **Genre Agnostic** — Fantasy, sci-fi, cyberpunk, western, horror, pirate, or custom. One platform, every world.

## Seven Genre Themes (cycling hero)
1. **Fantasy** — Magic systems, pantheons, ancient lore
2. **Sci-Fi** — Star systems, factions, alien species
3. **Cyberpunk** — Megacorps, street gangs, cyberware
4. **Western** — Frontier towns, outlaws, dust and grit ("The Noon Train Carries the Marshal")
5. **Horror** — Dread, mystery, things in the dark
6. **Pirate** — Ships, treasure, uncharted waters
7. **Your Own World** — Any setting, any rules

## Visual Identity
- **Palette**: Dark warm (brown-black backgrounds, teal/gold accents, parchment-toned text)
- **Fonts**: Cormorant Garamond serif headings + DM Sans body, genre-specific display fonts
- **Aesthetic**: "Fantasy-meets-technology" — illuminated manuscript built by engineers
- **Inspiration**: D&D Beyond (illustrated fantasy vibrancy) + Raycast/Stripe (polished modules, bento grids)
- **Feel**: Vibrant, illustrated, alive — not flat or corporate

## Target Audience
Game Masters, DMs, writers, world-builders who want creative control and AI assistance without AI overreach. The human is always in charge.

1. Immediately communicate what Luminth is and who it's for
2. Showcase genre versatility through animated hero cycling 7 genre themes
